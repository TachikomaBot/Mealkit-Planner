## 1. Japan's Bacon Okonomiyaki
*with Scallions & Furikake Mayo*

**Cooking Time:** 30 minutes

### Ingredients
- 100g Naturally smoked bacon (raised without antibiotics or Canadian farmers)
- 100g Coleslaw mix
- 2 Scallions
- 400g Sweet potatoes
- 30ml Mayonnaise
- 120g All-purpose flour
- 10g Mild Furikake (black sesame seeds, white sesame seeds, roasted nori, sugar, kosher salt)

### Steps

#### 1. MISE EN PLACE
- Thinly slice the **scallions** crosswise, separating the white bottoms and green tops.
- Peel and grate the **sweet potatoes**.
- Cut the **bacon slices** crosswise into thirds.

#### 2. PREPARE THE BATTER
- In a medium bowl, combine **1 egg**, **⅓ cup water** (double both for 4 portions), the **flour** and **S&P**.
- Add the **sweet potatoes**, **coleslaw mix**, **white bottoms of the scallions** and **½ the mild furikake**; gently fold until combined.

#### 3. PREPARE & COOK THE OKONOMIYAKI
- Working in batches if necessary, spoon the **batter** to form **4 round pancakes** (double for 4 portions) about **¾ inch thick**.
- In a large pan, heat a **thin layer of oil** on medium.
- Add the **pancakes** and top with the **bacon slices**, pressing lightly.
- Cook, **3 to 4 min. per side**, until golden.

#### 4. MAKE THE FURIKAKE MAYO
- In a second small bowl, combine the **mayo**, **1 tbsp water** (double for 4 portions) and the **remaining mild furikake**.

#### 5. PLATE YOUR DISH
- Divide the **okonomiyaki** between your plates.
- Garnish with the **green tops of the scallions**.
- Serve the **furikake mayo** on the side. Bon appétit!

### Image Notes

- **Step 2:** Bowl with mixed batter showing texture
- **Step 3:** Pan with golden pancakes topped with bacon, cooking
- **Step 4:** Small bowl with creamy white sauce speckled with furikake
- **Step 5:** Finished dish on blue plate with chopsticks, garnished with green scallions

## 2. Québécois-Style Pork Chops
*with Carrot Poutine*

**Cooking Time:** 25 minutes

### Ingredients
- 2 Pork chops
- 300g Nantes carrots
- 10g Cornstarch
- 12g Chicken demi-glace
- 150g Green peas
- 100g Cheese curds
- 8g Montpellier to Moscow spices (salt, vinegar, paprika, garlic, sugar, thyme, parsley, rosemary, green bell peppers, mustard, onion, Cayenne pepper, canola oil, bay leaves)

### Steps

#### 1. ROAST THE CARROTS
- Preheat the oven to **450°F**.
- Halve the **carrots** lengthwise (quarter if large).
- On a lined sheet pan, toss with a drizzle of **oil**, **½ the spices** and **S&P**.
- Roast, flipping halfway, **18 to 20 min.**, until tender.

#### 2. COOK THE PORK
- Meanwhile, pat the **pork** dry; season with **all but a pinch of the remaining spices** and **S&P**.
- In a medium pan, heat a drizzle of **oil** on medium-high.
- Add the **pork*** and cook, **3 to 5 min. per side**, until browned and cooked through.
- Transfer to a cutting board, leaving any browned bits in the pan. Reserve the pan. Let rest before slicing.

#### 3. MAKE THE GRAVY
- In a medium bowl, combine the **cornstarch**, **demi-glace**, **¾ cup water** (double for 4 portions) and **remaining spices**.
- In the reserved pan, heat **2 tbsp butter** (double for 4 portions) on medium.
- Add the **peas** and sauté, scraping up any browned bits, **2 to 3 min.**, until warmed through.
- Reduce to medium-low and add the **cornstarch mixture**. Simmer, stirring often, **30 sec. to 2 min.**, until the sauce thickens; season with **black pepper**.

#### 4. PLATE YOUR DISH
- Divide the **carrots** and **pork** between your plates.
- Top with the **gravy** and **cheese curds**. Bon appétit!

### Image Notes
- **Step 1:** Sheet pan with halved carrots, seasoned
- **Step 2:** Pan with pork chops cooking, golden brown
- **Step 3:** Pot with gravy and peas simmering
- **Step 4:** Plated dish with sliced pork, roasted carrots, peas, cheese curds, gravy
- **Hero image:** Overhead plate with sliced pork chops, roasted carrot fries, green peas, white cheese curds, on blue-rimmed plate

## 3. Beef Keema Vindaloo
*with Squash & Peas*

**Cooking Time:** 20 minutes

**Customizable with:** Impossible Beef, Ground Pork

### Ingredients
- 250g Canadian-raised lean ground beef
- 20g Ginger
- 200g Diced butternut squash
- 150g Green peas
- 56ml Tomato paste
- 190g Basmati rice
- 12g Beef demi-glace
- 12g Curry Favour spices (garlic purée, coriander, ginger purée, sea salt, paprika, cumin, turmeric)

### Steps

#### 1. COOK THE RICE
- In a medium pot, combine the **rice**, **1½ cups water** (double for 4 portions) and a big pinch of **salt**; bring to a boil.
- Reduce the heat, cover and simmer, **14 to 16 min.**, until the rice is tender and the water has been absorbed.
- Remove from the heat and let sit, covered, for **5 min.** Fluff the **rice**.

#### 2. COOK THE BEEF
- Meanwhile, peel and mince the **ginger**.
- In a second medium pot, heat a drizzle of **oil** on medium-high.
- Add the **beef***, **squash** and **ginger**; season with the **spices** and **S&P**.
- Cook, breaking up the meat, **4 to 6 min.**, until cooked through.
- → Ground pork: same instruction as above.
- → Impossible™ Beef: Crumble the patties. Cook, stirring often, **3 to 5 min.**

#### 3. MAKE THE VINDALOO
- To the pot of beef, add the **tomato paste** and cook, stirring often, **30 sec. to 1 min.**, until dark red.
- Add the **peas**, **demi-glace** and **½ cup water** (double for 4 portions).
- Cook, stirring occasionally, **3 to 4 min.**, until slightly reduced and thickened.

#### 4. PLATE YOUR DISH
- Divide the **rice** between your bowls.
- Top with the **vindaloo**. Bon appétit!

### Image Notes
- **Step 1:** Pot with cooked fluffy white rice
- **Step 2:** Pan with browned ground beef and orange squash cubes
- **Step 3:** Pan with completed vindaloo showing dark red sauce with peas
- **Step 4:** Bowl with white rice topped with beef vindaloo
- **Hero image:** Blue plate on blue background with fluffy basmati rice topped with beef keema vindaloo, showing orange squash and green peas

## 4. Cheese Tortellini with Sage Brown Butter
*Crisp Radish & Celery Salad*

**Cooking Time:** 10 minutes

### Ingredients
- 4g Sage
- 2 Garlic cloves
- 100g Radishes
- 1 Celery stalk
- 80g Baby greens (deluxe mix: baby spinach, kale or arugula)
- 350g Fresh three-cheese tortellini (contains: Gluten)
- 15ml Sherry vinegar
- 25g Grana Padano (contains: rennet)
- 60ml Heavy cream
- 10g Zesty Herbs spices (salt, garlic, dried carrots, onion, red bell pepper, sugar, celery powder, mustard, canola oil, green, black pepper, lemon oil, citric acid)

### Steps

#### 1. MISE EN PLACE
- Bring a medium pot of **salted water** to a boil.
- Cut the **radishes** into **⅛-inch wedges**.
- Thinly slice the **celery** crosswise on an angle.
- Pluck the **sage leaves** off the stems. Mince; slice the **leaves**.

#### 2. BOIL THE PASTA
- Add the **pasta** to the pot of boiling water; stir gently to separate.
- Boil, stirring occasionally, **3 to 5 min.**, until tender.
- Reserving **¼ cup cooking water** (double for 4 portions), drain and toss with a drizzle of **oil** to prevent sticking.

#### 3. MAKE THE SAUCE
- Meanwhile, mince the **garlic**.
- In a large pan, heat **4 tbsp butter** (double for 4 portions).
- Cook, swirling the pan often, **2 to 3 min.**, until the butter foams and turns a deep golden.
- Add the **garlic** and **sage** (be careful as it may sputter) and cook, **30 sec. to 1 min.**, until fragrant.
- Add the **cream** and ½ the **reserved cooking water**. Bring to a boil. Reduce to a simmer.

#### 4. FINISH THE SAUCE & COMBINE THE PASTA
- To the pan, add the **spinach**, **pasta**, **lemon juice** and **½ the cheese**.
- Cook, stirring often, **1 to 2 min.**, until combined and the spinach has wilted; season with **S&P**.
- If the sauce seems dry, gradually add the **remaining cooking water** until you achieve your desired consistency.

#### 5. MAKE THE SALAD & SERVE
- In a large bowl, combine the **vinegar**, **2 tbsp oil** (double for 4 portions), the **remaining spices** and **S&P**.
- Add the **baby greens**, **celery** and **radishes**; toss well.
- Divide the **pasta** between your plates.
- Garnish with the **remaining cheese**.
- Serve the **salad** on the side. Bon appétit!

### Image Notes
- **Step 1:** Cutting board with sliced radishes, celery, sage
- **Step 2:** Pot with boiling tortellini
- **Step 3:** Pan with brown butter, sage leaves visible
- **Step 4:** Pan with creamy pasta and wilted greens
- **Step 5:** Plated tortellini with side salad of radishes and greens
- **Hero image:** Green ceramic plates with creamy tortellini, grated cheese, side salad, lemon wedge, on pink tablecloth

## 5. Cheesy Caramelized Onion Beef Burgers
*with Balsamic Baby Greens Salad*

**Cooking Time:** 15 minutes

**Customizable with:** Ground Pork, Impossible Beef

### Ingredients
- 250g Canadian-raised lean ground beef
- 1 Cucumber
- 80g Baby greens (deluxe mix: baby spinach, kale or arugula)
- 30ml Balsamic vinegar
- 25g Caramelized onions
- 30g Grated aged cheddar
- 2 Artisan hamburger buns
- 8g Montreal Steak House spices (sea salt, garlic, onion, mustard, coriander seeds, thyme, canola oil)

### Steps

#### 1. PREPARE THE PATTIES
- In a large bowl, combine the **beef**, **onions**, **spices** and **S&P**.
- Form into **2 patties** (double for 4 portions).
- → Ground pork: same instruction as above.
- → Impossible™ Beef: crumble the patties. Same instruction as above.

#### 2. COOK THE PATTIES
- In a large pan, heat a drizzle of **oil** on medium-high.
- Add the **patties*** and cook, **3 to 5 min. per side**, until cooked through.
- Top with the **cheese**.
- Cover and cook, **1 to 2 min.**, until melted.
- Transfer to a plate and reserve the pan.
- → Ground pork: same instruction as above.
- → Impossible™ Beef: **2 to 4 min. per side**.

#### 3. MAKE THE SALAD
- Meanwhile, halve the **cucumber** lengthwise; thinly slice crosswise on an angle.
- In a medium bowl, combine the **vinegar** (start with ½), **2 tbsp oil** (double for 4 portions) and **S&P**.
- Add the **baby greens** and **cucumber**; toss well.

#### 4. TOAST THE BUNS
- In the reserved pan, heat a drizzle of **oil** on medium.
- Add the **buns**, cut-sides down, and toast, **1 to 2 min.**, until warmed through and beginning to brown.

#### 5. PLATE YOUR DISH
- Divide the **bun bottoms** between your plates.
- Top each bun bottom with a **patty**, a spoonful of the **salad** and a **bun top**.
- Serve the **remaining salad** on the side. Bon appétit!

### Image Notes
- **Step 1:** Raw beef patties on plate
- **Step 2:** Pan with patties cooking, topped with melting cheese
- **Step 3:** Bowl with cucumber slices and greens
- **Step 4:** Pan with buns toasting, golden brown
- **Step 5:** Assembled burgers with cucumber, salad on side
- **Hero image:** Two assembled burgers on white plates with blue stripe, cucumber slices visible, baby greens salad on side, balsamic dressing in small jar

## 6. Crispy Chicken Kyiv
*with Chive Butter & Green Beans*

**Cooking Time:** 30 minutes

**Customizable with:** Chicken Thighs

### Ingredients
- 2 Canadian-raised chicken breasts (air chilled)
- 200g Green beans (or string beans)
- 8g Chives (or garlic chives)
- 2 Garlic cloves
- 60g Panko
- 140g Orzo
- 15ml Dijon mustard
- 20g All-purpose flour

### Steps

#### 1. PREPARE THE CHICKEN
- Preheat the oven to **450°F**. Bring a medium pot of **salted water** to a boil.
- Pat the **chicken** dry; season with **S&P**.
- Place the **flour** in a shallow bowl; season with **S&P**.
- In a second shallow bowl, beat **1 egg** (double for 4 portions) and the **mustard** until smooth; season with **S&P**.
- Place the **panko** in a third shallow bowl.
- Working one at a time, coat the **chicken** in the **flour** (shaking off any excess), then in the **egg** (letting any excess drip off), then in the **panko** (pressing to adhere).
- Arrange on a lined sheet pan.

#### 2. START THE CHICKEN
- Roast the **chicken**, **13 to 15 min.**, until partially cooked.
- → Chicken thighs: same instruction as above.

#### 3. BOIL THE ORZO
- Meanwhile, add the **orzo** to the pot of boiling water and boil, **9 to 10 min.**, until just tender.
- Drain and rinse.
- Transfer to a bowl and toss with a drizzle of **oil** to prevent sticking.
- Reserve the pot.

#### 4. ROAST THE GREEN BEANS & FINISH THE CHICKEN
- Meanwhile, remove the stem ends of the **green beans**. In a medium bowl, toss with a drizzle of **oil** and **S&P**.
- When the chicken is partially cooked, add the **green beans** and roast, flipping halfway, **7 to 9 min.**, until the **chicken*** is cooked through and the green beans are tender.
- → Chicken thighs: same instruction as above.

#### 5. MAKE THE CHIVE BUTTER
- Mince the **garlic**.
- Thinly slice the **chives**.
- In the reserved pot, heat **2 tbsp butter** (double for 4 portions) on medium.
- Add the **garlic** and sauté, **30 sec. to 1 min.**, until fragrant.
- Off the heat, add the **chives** and **S&P**; stir well.

#### 6. FINISH & SERVE
- To the bowl of orzo, add **½ the chive butter**; stir well.
- Divide the **orzo**, **chicken** (slice beforehand if desired) and **green beans** between your plates.
- Drizzle the chicken with the **remaining chive butter**. Bon appétit!

### Image Notes
- **Step 1:** Raw chicken breasts being breaded, three bowls with flour, egg, panko
- **Step 2:** Breaded chicken on sheet pan, partially cooked
- **Step 3:** Pot with cooked orzo pasta
- **Step 4:** Sheet pan with chicken and green beans roasting together
- **Step 5:** Small pot with melted butter, chives, garlic
- **Step 6:** Plated dish with sliced crispy chicken, orzo, green beans
- **Hero image:** Pink/coral plate with sliced crispy chicken showing golden crust, chive butter drizzled, orzo pasta, roasted green beans, on grey textured background

## 7. Creamy Unstuffed Fresh Pasta Shells
*with Artichokes & Leafy Greens*

**Cooking Time:** 15 minutes

### Ingredients
- 1 Lemon
- 90g Baby greens (deluxe mix: baby spinach, kale or arugula)
- 50g Diced onions
- 15g Minced roasted garlic
- 225g Fresh conchiglie pasta
- 170ml Marinated artichokes (jar)
- 80ml Heavy cream
- 25g Grana Padano (contains: rennet)

### Steps

#### 1. BOIL THE PASTA
- Bring a medium pot of **salted water** to a boil.
- Add the **pasta**; stir gently to separate.
- Boil, **6 to 8 min.**, until al dente.
- Reserving **½ cup cooking water** (double for 4 portions), drain and toss with a drizzle of **oil** to prevent sticking.

#### 2. MISE EN PLACE
- Meanwhile, halve the **lemon**; juice **½** and quarter the **remaining ½**.
- Drain the **artichokes** and pat dry. Chop.

#### 3. START THE SAUCE
- In a large pan, heat a drizzle of **oil** on medium.
- Add the **onions** and sauté, **2 to 3 min.**, until fragrant.
- Add the **garlic** and **artichokes**. Sauté, **2 to 3 min.**, until fragrant.
- Add the **cream** and ½ the **reserved cooking water**. Cook, stirring occasionally, **2 to 3 min.**, until slightly thickened.

#### 4. FINISH THE SAUCE & COMBINE THE PASTA
- To the pan, add the **spinach**, **pasta**, **lemon juice** and **½ the cheese**.
- Cook, stirring often, **1 to 2 min.**, until combined and the spinach has wilted; season with **S&P**.
- If the sauce seems dry, gradually add the **remaining cooking water** until you achieve your desired consistency.

#### 5. PLATE YOUR DISH
- Divide the **pasta** between your plates.
- Garnish with the **lemon wedges** and **remaining cheese**. Bon appétit!

### Image Notes
- **Step 1:** Pot with shell pasta boiling
- **Step 2:** Cutting board with lemon halved, artichokes drained
- **Step 3:** Pan with onions, garlic, artichokes sautéing
- **Step 4:** Pan with creamy pasta, wilted greens mixed in
- **Step 5:** Red plate with creamy pasta shells, lemon wedge garnish
- **Hero image:** Red ceramic plate with creamy unstuffed pasta shells, visible artichokes and wilted greens, grated cheese on top, lemon wedge, on blue patterned background

## 8. Korean Spicy Beef Udon Noodles
*with Kimchi-Apple Salad & Peanuts*

**Cooking Time:** 10 minutes

**Customizable with:** Ground Pork

### Ingredients
- 250g Canadian-raised lean ground beef
- 340g Baby bok choy
- 55g Green apple slices
- 35g Organic kimchi
- 15g Gochujang
- 56ml Tomato paste
- 225g Fresh udon noodles
- 25g Chopped peanuts
- 12g Mellow Sesame spices (brown sugar, sea salt, white sesame, garlic, canola oil, ginger, paprika flavour, black pepper, coriander)

### Steps

#### 1. BOIL THE NOODLES
- Bring a medium pot of **salted water** to a boil.
- Add the **noodles**; stir gently to separate.
- Boil, **6 to 8 min.**, until al dente.
- Reserving **½ cup cooking water** (double for 4 portions), drain and toss with a drizzle of **oil** to prevent sticking.

#### 2. COOK THE BEEF & BOK CHOY
- Meanwhile, remove the root ends of the **bok choy**; roughly chop.
- In a large pan, heat a drizzle of **oil** on medium-high.
- Add the **tomato paste** and **gochujang**. Cook, stirring often, **1 to 2 min.**, until dark red.
- Add the **beef**; season with **all but a pinch of the spices**. Cook, breaking up the meat, **2 to 3 min.**, until partially cooked.
- Add the **bok choy** and cook, stirring often, **2 min.**, until the **beef*** is cooked through and the bok choy is tender.
- → Ground pork: same instruction as above.

#### 3. MAKE THE SALAD
- Meanwhile, roughly chop the **kimchi**.
- In a medium bowl, combine the **apple**, **kimchi**, a drizzle of **oil**, the **remaining spices** and **S&P**.

#### 4. COMBINE THE NOODLES
- To the pan, add the **noodles** and **½ the reserved cooking water**.
- Cook, stirring occasionally, **1 to 2 min.**, until combined; season with **S&P**.
- If the sauce seems dry, gradually add the **remaining cooking water** until you achieve your desired consistency.

#### 5. PLATE YOUR DISH
- Divide the **noodles** between your bowls.
- Garnish with the **peanuts**.
- Serve the **salad** on the side. Bon appétit!

### Image Notes
- **Step 1:** Pot with udon noodles boiling
- **Step 2:** Pan with ground beef, bok choy, red sauce
- **Step 3:** Bowl with green apple slices and red kimchi
- **Step 4:** Pan with noodles combined with beef mixture
- **Step 5:** Bowl with noodles, topped with peanuts, salad on side
- **Hero image:** Black bowls on dark background with spicy udon noodles, visible beef and bok choy, small bowl of chopped peanuts, kimchi-apple salad in separate bowl

# Format Analysis

## Recipe Card Structure

### Front of Card
- **Logo:** GoodFood bird logo (red)
- **Title:** Recipe name in teal/turquoise bold caps
- **Subtitle:** "with [accompaniments]" in grey
- **Hero Image:** Large overhead shot of finished dish, styled
- **Description:** 3-4 sentences about the dish
- **Cooking Time:** Clock icon with "X MINUTES"
- **Customization Badge:** (if applicable) "CUSTOMIZABLE WITH" + protein options
- **Ingredients List:** Bulleted, with weights in grams/ml

### Back of Card
- **"YOU'LL NEED" section:** Equipment/pantry staples
- **6 Steps Grid:** 2 rows of 3 steps each
- **Each Step:**
  - Step image (overhead, process shot)
  - Numbered title in teal caps (e.g., "1. MISE EN PLACE")
  - Checkbox bullets for each instruction
  - **Bold** ingredients inline
  - Cooking times in bold
  - Scaling notes in parentheses "(double for 4 portions)"
  - Alternative protein instructions with arrow "→"

## Styling Patterns

### Text Formatting
- **Bold:** All ingredients when mentioned in instructions
- **Bold:** Cooking times and temperatures
- Regular: Action verbs and connecting text
- Asterisk (*): Indicates protein with cooking temp note at bottom

### Step Naming Conventions
1. MISE EN PLACE / PREP
2. Action verbs: COOK, ROAST, BOIL, PREPARE
3. MAKE THE [SAUCE/SALAD/etc.]
4. FINISH / COMBINE
5. PLATE YOUR DISH
6. (Promotional content or additional step)

### Image Style Notes (for future AI generation)
- **Overhead angle** for most shots
- **Natural lighting** with soft shadows
- **Props:** Wooden cutting boards, matching ceramic plates, linen napkins
- **Color palette:** Teal accents, warm wood tones, colorful ingredients
- **Composition:** Centered dish, partial props at edges
- **Process shots:** Show actual cooking state (bubbling, browning, etc.)
